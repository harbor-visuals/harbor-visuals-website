    Projects

    Music Video: Maggie

    As part of the final project for a video production course at SAE Zurich, my group and I collaborated on the music video “Maggie” for the Swiss singer-songwriter Linda Elys. Taking charge of the camera work and serving as Director of Photography, I played a pivotal role in the planning and execution stages. Additionally, I led the post-production efforts, overseeing editing and color grading alongside a fellow student. Our collective vision and dedication resulted in a visually stunning and harmonious music video experience.

    Floral Layne: The Journey

    Capturing the essence of artist Floral Layne's journey from California to Switzerland, this video serves as a introduction to her story. Delving into her relocation prompted by love, the footage offers a glimpse into her origins and the path that led her to Switzerland. Designed as a captivating welcome for website visitors, the video embodies Floral Layne's unique narrative and sets the stage for an immersive exploration of her artistry.

    For this video the goal was to capture the magic of Floral Layne's Christmas market stall. From twinkling lights to joyful laughter, experience the warmth and charm of the season as we preserve this cherished memory for you to relive again and again.

    Travel Videos

    For all my trips, I capture travel videos, showcasing foreign places and their landmarks. Each video is a visual diary, allowing viewers to immerse themselves in the beauty of each destination.

    Nature Videos

    Indulge in the wonder of nature with our captivating videos, inspired by the renowned BBC documentaries. Drawing from my fascination with the natural world, these videos offer a glimpse into the breathtaking landscapes and fascinating wildlife that grace our planet.
