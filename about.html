    About

    Hello, I'm Philippe Hafen!

    My Story

    Greetings and thank you for visiting my website.

    Allow me to introduce myself. I am Philippe Hafen, a 24-year-old professional based near Basel, Switzerland. Following the completion of my commercial apprenticeship six years ago, I have dedicated myself to IT-related work and ongoing studies.

    My passion for photography and videography has been a cornerstone of my life for many years. Recently, I have taken steps to enhance my skills in video production by completing a specialized course, aiming to broaden my knowledge and expertise. Currently, I am furthering my education in Web Design & Development at SAE Zurich.

    Over nearly nine years, I have embarked on a journey of exploration through the lens of my camera, capturing the beauty and diversity of our planet. Through my work in photography and videography, I aim to share these experiences with others.

    Each image and video I produce tells its own unique story, evoking a range of emotions in those who view them. This passion for visual storytelling has naturally led me to explore Web and Graphic Design, where I have gained a profound appreciation for the transformative impact of design on online experiences.

    Social media serves as an essential platform, enabling me to connect with individuals across the globe and share my creative projects.

    Should you have any inquiries or simply wish to connect, please do not hesitate to reach out via the contact form or email. I am always eager to engage with new individuals and explore collaborative opportunities.
